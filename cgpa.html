How it works

Common questions
What should I enter in each row?

Enter one completed semester GPA or SGPA and its total credits in each row.

Can I calculate CGPA from semester SGPA values?

Yes. GPA and SGPA are used the same way here for semester-wise entries, as long as you also enter the correct credits.

Can I skip a semester row?

Yes. Blank rows are ignored, but partially filled rows are flagged.

How is CGPA calculated?

CGPA is the total weighted GPA points divided by the total completed credits.

Should I include the current ongoing semester?

Only include a semester if you already know its GPA and completed credits.

What if two semesters have the same GPA?

That is fine. Each row is calculated using its own GPA and credits.

Why is my CGPA different from a simple average?

CGPA is credit-weighted, so semesters with more credits affect the final number more.

FAQ

Quick answers

What is SGPA?

SGPA is semester grade point average. It is calculated from subject credits and grade points for one term.

What is CGPA?

CGPA combines all completed semester results, weighted by credits, to show the overall academic average.

How are backlogs handled?

F, Ab, W, and I are treated as 0 points in this calculator so students can see the effect clearly.

Can I share my result?

Yes. Each calculator includes copy and share actions so you can save or send the result quickly.

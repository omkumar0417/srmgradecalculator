    FAQ

    GPA calculator questions

    What do blank rows do?

    Blank rows are ignored. That makes it easy to add extra rows before you finish entering subjects.

    Can I use backlog grades?

    Yes. F, Ab, W, and I are treated as zero points so their effect is visible in the result.

    Can I share the GPA?

    Yes. Use the copy or share buttons to send a text summary of your calculation.
